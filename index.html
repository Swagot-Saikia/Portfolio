About

I'm Swagot — a 20-year-old from Assam studying Information Technology, but that's a thin description of what I actually am. I'm the person who gets equally excited about a shot composition and a clean algorithm.

My orbit: AI, filmmaking, photography, robotics, animation, and the politics that shape all of it. I shoot on a Canon EOS 1500D and I think in frames — whether I'm writing code or walking a street looking for a shot.

I'm neither a pure engineer nor a pure artist. I'm building the space between. Every project I make lives at that intersection — and that's where I intend to stay.

I'm at an early stage, and I know it. This portfolio is a live document of the work I'm doing to close the gap between what I imagine and what I can ship.
